Privacy Policy

This extension collects:

  • Discord username and user ID (for linking stats)
  • Balatrodle game statistics (wins, streaks, average guesses for a win)

Data is sent to a Discord webhook and not stored elsewhere.

No personal information, browsing history, or other data is collected.

Users can uninstall the extension to stop data collection.
